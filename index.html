STRADDLE RULES
HIGHER WIN RATE — WAIT FOR THE FAKE OUT FIRST
1
Let the news drop at 2:30 PM Nigeria time. Do NOT enter immediately. Spreads are wide and price will fake out first.
2
Watch the first 5-minute candle after news. Wait for it to fully close. This candle shows you the real direction after the fake out.
3
Enter in the direction of that closed candle. If it closed bullish — BUY at the open of the next candle. If bearish — SELL.
4
Stop Loss: below the wick of that 5-min candle. For NFP on Gold — minimum 60 pip SL. News candles have wide wicks.
5
Take Profit: TP1 at 80 pips, TP2 at 150 pips for Gold. TP1 at 50 pips, TP2 at 100 pips for GBP/USD and EUR/USD. These are NFP average ranges.
6
After TP1 hits — move SL to break-even immediately. Let the rest run. NFP moves often continue for 2–4 hours after release.
IF YOU HAVE AN OPEN SMC TRADE WHEN NEWS IS APPROACHING
IF YOUR TRADE IS IN LOSS
Close it immediately. Do not hold a losing trade through high-impact news. News will not save a bad trade — it will make it worse. Take the small loss now before the spread widens and your loss doubles.
IF YOUR TRADE IS AT BREAK-EVEN OR SMALL PROFIT (under 20 pips)
Move SL to break-even and reduce position size by 50%. You are now protected. If news kills the trade you lose nothing. If news pushes your direction you still profit.
IF YOUR TRADE IS IN GOOD PROFIT (20–50 pips up)
Close 50% of your position to lock in profit. Move SL on the remaining 50% to break-even. You have now guaranteed a profitable trade regardless of what news does.
IF YOUR TRADE IS IN STRONG PROFIT (50+ pips up)
Close 75% of your position and move SL to lock in at least 30 pips on the remaining 25%. Let it ride. If news continues in your direction you collect the extra bonus. This is the best position to be in.
